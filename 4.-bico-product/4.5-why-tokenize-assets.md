# 4.5 Why Tokenize Assets ?

* **Global Reach :** Invest in assets from anywhere in the world, 24/7.&#x20;
* **Enhanced Transparency :** Blockchain technology ensures that every transaction is recorded on a public ledger, providing complete transparency.&#x20;
* **Increased Liquidity :** Tokenized assets can be traded instantly on various cryptocurrency exchanges.&#x20;
* **Cost-Effective :** Tokenization eliminates intermediaries and reduces transaction costs.
